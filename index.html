About us

Fauna helps pets that are still in shelters or that have gone missing, but it doesn't imply that Fauna doesn't want to assist owners as well.

"You become responsible forever for what you've tamed.” ― Antoine de Saint-Exupéry, The Little Prince.

That's why you take responsibility for what you have tamed, your pets would recognize the same color of your hair, the way you smell, or even the way you walk, just because it's you. The same thing happens to us as humans too often. That's life, something that we can't predict. But it does not matter until you know that taming is the act of forming ties. And your pets tamed you just as much as you tamed them.

Fauna is Interested in helping your pet AND you.
